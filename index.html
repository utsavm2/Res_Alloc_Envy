Optimizing Resource Allocation under Envy Constraints

Motivation

There are many real-world scenarios where one would want to assign tasks to agents in such a way that it minimizes the overall cost. However, one also has to take into account every agent's individual preference for a particular task in order to maintain high morale and long-term sustainable performance. We tackle this by balancing minimization of cost and minimizing envy in the system.
